\subsection{Package}
The \href{https://github.com/eybmits/qc_symmetry}{project repository}
contains the material needed to reproduce the paper: experiment code, checked
result tables, figure-generation scripts, and manuscript sources.

\subsection{Data generation}
Following Meyer et al.~\cite{meyer2023symmetry}, we enumerate the 5478 legal,
reachable boards, stop at terminal states, count unfinished positions as draws,
and use class-balanced splits with a fixed 600-board test set. The stored
tables record sampled indices, seeds, model family, subgroup, interactions,
parameter count, and training size.
