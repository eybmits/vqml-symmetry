\noindent\textbf{Data generation and sampling.}
Following Meyer et al.~\cite{meyer2023symmetry}, we enumerate the 5478 legal,
reachable boards by expanding the game tree and stopping at terminal states;
unfinished positions count as draws. Splits are class-balanced; the 600-board
test set is fixed and shared across models.

\noindent\textbf{Reproducibility.}
The accompanying
\href{https://anonymous.4open.science/r/qc_symmetry-5FB4/README.md}{anonymous review repository}
contains the code, checked result tables, figure-generation scripts, and
manuscript sources needed to reproduce the reported results, plots, and short
paper. This makes the full workflow, from experiment outputs to the final PDF,
directly auditable and reproducible.
